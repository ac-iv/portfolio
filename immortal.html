don't worry dear, i am immortal

mortality and legacy through font design

don't worry dear, i am immortal is a personal exploration of mortality and legacy through the lens of font design. the project sits with the idea that while our lives are finite, creativity has the power to transcend time — by designing a font that mimics handwriting, the work symbolically preserves a part of its creator's identity for future generations.

the book captures a journey of self-reflection, where the act of creation becomes a statement of defiance against the passage of time. an accompanying poem serves as a meditation on life, creation, and the human desire to leave an indelible mark — a celebration of how art and design can keep one's spirit alive long after they're gone, making the intangible tangible.
