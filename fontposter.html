012 — typography series2024

font posters

three typefaces, three temperaments

this series explores three very different typefaces — cooper black, neue haas grotesk, and memphis — and how their individual voices can drive composition. each poster is built around the character of the font itself, letting weight, proportion, and rhythm dictate layout rather than forcing a fixed system.

the approach is intentionally minimal. typography does the work, with small shifts in scale, spacing, and color used to create tension and clarity. the goal isn't decoration, but attention — treating type as a material with tone, attitude, and presence.

cooper black leans into softness and excess, using density and scale to emphasize warmth and confidence. neue haas grotesk is handled with restraint, focusing on alignment and balance to reflect its neutrality and precision. memphis sits somewhere in between, playing with structure and contrast while maintaining order.

together, the posters function as quiet studies in how typography shapes meaning — not just through what it says, but through how it feels.
