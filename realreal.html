the realreal

web banners that earn the glance

a series of web banners designed for the realreal's online service. the goal was banners that grab attention while communicating the essence of the service clearly and effectively — crafted to enhance engagement and keep the experience seamless for visitors.

the approach was user-centered: clarity and visual appeal first, so the message lands at a glance. layouts were designed with flexibility in mind to work across screen sizes and formats, and every banner aligns with the brand's tone through consistent color, type, and visual elements.
