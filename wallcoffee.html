wall-mounted coffee machine

the counter, reclaimed

a product design concept for a wall-mounted coffee machine — moving the morning ritual off the counter and onto the wall, like a fixture rather than an appliance.

the design studies mounting, water, and heat as constraints, and treats the machine's silhouette as part of the kitchen's architecture.
