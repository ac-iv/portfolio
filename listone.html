listone

one listing, every marketplace

listone was designed to solve a very specific frustration: having to manually post the same resale item across multiple marketplaces. the platform allows users to sign into their resale accounts in one place and publish a single listing that automatically syndicates across platforms.

the project focused on reducing redundant work for resellers by creating a centralized control layer. instead of jumping between apps, users manage titles, photos, prices, and shipping logic inside one streamlined interface.

year — 2024
role — product strategy, ui/ux, systems design
