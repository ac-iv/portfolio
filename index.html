fonefax

a speculative project merging retro fax technology with the smartphone — converting digital messages into tangible, physical outputs. in a world of cold glass screens, fonefax reclaims thoughtful communication: messages you can hold, share, and keep. convenience and meaning can coexist.

don't worry dear, i'm immortal

a personal exploration of mortality and legacy through font design. a handwriting font symbolically preserves its creator's identity beyond the boundaries of life — accompanied by a poem meditating on creation, the desire to leave a mark, and how art keeps a spirit alive.
