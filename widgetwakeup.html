widget wakeup

a better first thirty seconds of your day

widget wakeup is a customizable widget that enhances the morning routine by integrating alarms, weather updates, and motivational quotes into one sleek design. the concept gives users a more interactive, productive start to the day by streamlining essential morning tasks into a single, elegant widget.

the layout is fully customizable — color schemes, fonts, and content types like calendar events and reminders. smart alarm integration adapts to sleep patterns and surfaces important tasks on waking, while real-time weather, quotes, and reminders keep the widget useful all morning.
