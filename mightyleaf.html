mighty leaf

packaging as a statement of values

a packaging redesign for mighty leaf tea that balances elegance and eco-consciousness, marrying aesthetics with functionality. inspired by the natural ingredients and the brand's commitment to sustainability, the new design reduces waste without compromising the visual appeal tea lovers cherish.

the updated look features clear, modern lines that highlight the purity of the product inside. recyclable materials underscore the brand's dedication to the environment, while streamlined visuals make each blend easily distinguishable. it isn't just a fresh look — it's a statement of values, innovation, and a commitment to a more sustainable future.
