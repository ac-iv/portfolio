ketchup

a social app for people who love food

ketchup is a food-focused social media app that brings people together through shared culinary experiences. it lets users discover and review restaurants, share photos, and engage with food trends in one elegant, user-friendly platform — a community where food lovers connect, recommend their favorite spots, and build visual stories around their dining experiences.

the design is clean and intuitive, with a minimalist interface that highlights user-generated content without overwhelming visuals. the experience is responsive across devices, and the community features are built for real-time reviews, photos, and stories.

the brand identity extends down to a custom app icon — simple and clean, reflecting food and community without cartoonish elements, keeping the product professional yet approachable.

year — 2024
role — ui/ux design, branding, graphic design
